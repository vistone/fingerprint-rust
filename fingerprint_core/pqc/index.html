Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC) Detection

Provides detection and fingerprinting of post-quantum cryptographic algorithms including Kyber (key encapsulation), Dilithium (digital signatures), and hybrid modes.

Based on NIST PQC standardization (2024) and modern TLS 1.3 extensions.
